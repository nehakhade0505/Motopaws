Our Story

Moto Paws was founded by Shantanu Naidu in Pune, India, with the goal of preventing road accidents involving stray dogs by fitting them with reflective collars which would reflect light at night time - making it easier to spot them . Naidu was inspired to start the organization after witnessing many dogs being hit by cars. After writing a heartfelt letter to Ratan Tata, Naidu received funding and support from him, which helped the initiative expand to many cities across multiple countries.
