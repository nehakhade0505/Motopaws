Events and Initiatives

Monthly Collaring Drives

Monthly collaring drives held across city chapters, where volunteers collar stray dogs with reflective collars.

Anti-Rabies Vaccination

Conducted with support from NGOs & welfare groups to help serve public health and ensure dog wellbeing.

Rescues & Rehabilitation

Volunteers are trained to handle medical rescues and support animals in distress.

Milestones

Geographic Reach

Motopaws operates in 11 Indian cities and has expanded to Nepal and Malaysia.

Volunteers

A network that grew from 15 founders to 200 passionate volunteers.

Collars Distributed

Over 4,000 collars distributed by partner organizations by 2024.

Funding

Initially, 70% of the production costs were supported by Ratan Tata. After he independently funded the project, with the remaining 30% from volunteers.

Visibility

The collars are designed with excellent retro-reflective material, increasing visibility of up to 1,000 feet to motorists at night.

Data Collection

We emphasize gathering data on collared animals and have tested using unique QR identifiers on the collars to track their location, & details.
